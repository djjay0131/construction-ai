### Notes

**Note 1 — U_NUM mismatch in `activeContext.md`:**
The activeContext file's HW5 block says "U_NUM = 0.063% at N=20", which
references the *parametric* w_max-relative U_NUM at the validation grid for
the standalone HW5 stand-alone study, not the final-project value (0.28%).
The final-project value 0.28% comes from the wider uncertainty space and
is correct in the report. activeContext.md should be patched in a follow-up
to disambiguate; not blocking for submission since memory bank is internal.

**Note 2 — AVM/MAVM divergence in `activeContext.md`:**
The activeContext file's HW5 block reports AVM=7.59e-3, MAVM=7.29e-3 for
"Dataset 1". Those are the **standalone HW5** values for Dataset 1 (χ_1
strongly biased). The final-project §6.4 uses a **project-specific
experimental CDF** that differs by construction, with AVM=2.139e-3 and
MAVM=1.079e-3. The report explicitly notes this divergence in §6.4. Not a
contradiction — different datasets, both reported correctly in their
respective contexts.
